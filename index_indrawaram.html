About Us

A little piece of the South, open all day.

The Indrawaram Cafe was built to bring the warmth of a South Indian kitchen, filter coffee brewed slow, dosas made to order, thalis served the traditional way, to a city that never quite had a place like it. Our name nods to Indrawaram, a small town in Andhra Pradesh known for exactly this kind of unhurried hospitality.

We kept the kitchen open around the clock on purpose. Whether it is a 7 AM filter coffee or a 2 AM dosa craving, there is always a table and a hot tawa waiting.
